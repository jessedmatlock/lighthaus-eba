Features & Benefits

The following is a complete list of all of the privileges EBA members enjoy.

  • DIRECTORY LISTING

    Be featured in our online network arena and connect with other top level business leaders and executives.

  • VIRTUAL ASSISTANT

    Enjoy the convenience of a Alliance Specialist that will help you make the most of your membership.

  • E-ANNOUNCEMENTS

    Promote yourself, your business, or an event with an announcement in one of our monthly e-announcements.

  • EXCLUSIVE CONTENT

    Get access to helpful articles and videos on a variety of business development and networking topics.

  • DESK ART / PLAQUE

    Commemorative desk art or wall plaque with your name and the EBA logo that you can display in your office.

  • GIFT SOLUTIONS

    Take advantage of exclusive offers and discounts to a variety of leading online services and shopping.

  • CHARITIES

    Choose from a variety of charities that will receive a donation from a part of your membership dues.

  • MENTORING

    Become a mentor to junior executive and pay it forward to the next generation of business leaders.

  • MEETINGS & EVENTS

    Access to exclusive regional EBA Chapter meetings as well as the Annual EBA Trade Show Conference.
